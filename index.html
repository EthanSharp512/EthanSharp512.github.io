About me

I'm Ethan and I'm a Software Engineer

After years of working in the pharmaceutical industry and taking courses to complete my undergrad prerequisites, I made the decision to switch career paths into the tech field. I found a passion for software development because I naturally love to create. I love art, movies, music, and video games and I have always been fascinated with making things from the ground up. In my free time, I am usually making music and finding ways to use my creativity to bring my ideas to life. For me, there is a direct parallel to making my art and coding, which is where my inclination for programming comes from. I was a very determined self-taught developer and I learned the basics from starting my own projects and researching Python. I then made the decision to quit my job and start at a full-time coding boot camp called DevMountain, based in Lehi, Utah. Over the span of 4 months, doing 8+ hours of coding a day, I strengthened my foundational knowledge as well as picked up new skills while specializing in Python. The switch from working in the pharmacy to becoming a full-stack software engineer was a huge change, but I found a path that I am passionate about and I have been much happier with my career ever since.
